The Beginning

The beginning from our company Tanaka Guitar it's start from selling variety kinds of guitars in offline store in the city of Tangerang. We start our business by adding guitar products one by one in our store so hopefully it might be can make the customers interested to buy it. Our store open at 09:00 AM - 22:00 PM every day and also we selling guitar start from IDR 75,000 - IDR 1,500,000.

The Journey

Then the journey from our business to selling the guitars is increasing by opening more store branches in Tangerang City. We already have about 5 branches in Tangerang and hopefully we can open more branches store again outside the Tangerang City, so people can get to know more about our guitar store.

Now

For now, we have made some progress that improve our business processes to selling the guitars which is we already build some application that people can download and use it to view and select our guitars also buy it where the price of each guitars is already listed in the application. So through our application, we hope can advertise our products in addition to selling them so people can know more about it and interested that can make our business successful.
